Research callouts — 4 visual treatments

The shipped version (variant A below) is on staging now. Three alternatives shown for comparison — all use the same Step 7 (Style & bio) content + Norcross & Lambert citation so you can A/B them at a glance.

Purpose of the feature: motivate therapists to enter honest, detailed, thoughtful information on signup steps 3–8. The framing is "your honest input means better matches FOR YOU" with a peer-reviewed citation to back the claim — not "fill these out to continue." Tone is collaborative, not lecturing.

A · shipped

Peach gradient with coral border

Always visible. Stands out clearly from the form. Currently live on staging.

Why we ask: Therapeutic alliance — the patient's sense of fit with you — accounts for roughly 30% of treatment outcome variance. That's more than the specific modality you use. Your bio and style tags are the patient's first signal of alliance fit. Write to your real style, not a marketing voice.

Source: Norcross & Lambert (2019), Psychotherapy Relationships That Work, 3rd ed.
